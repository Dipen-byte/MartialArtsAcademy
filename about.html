About Us

Our Story, Mission & Values

Our Story

Founded in 1999, Martial Arts Academy began with a simple mission: to make authentic martial arts training accessible to everyone.

What started as a small dojo with just 20 students has grown into one of London's premier martial arts facilities. Over the past 25 years, we have trained over 5,000 students, from complete beginners to professional competitors.

Our approach combines traditional martial arts values with modern training methodologies. We believe that martial arts is not just about physical combat—it's about personal development, mental discipline, and building a supportive community.

Our Core Values

  • Excellence: We strive for the highest standards in instruction and facility quality
  • Inclusivity: Martial arts is for everyone, regardless of age, gender, or fitness level
  • Safety: We maintain rigorous safety standards and injury prevention protocols
  • Community: We foster mutual respect and support among all members
  • Continuous Improvement: We constantly evolve our curriculum and facilities
