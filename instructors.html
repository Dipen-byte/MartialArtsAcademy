Grandmaster Robert Sterling

Founder & Chief Instructor

With over 40 years of martial arts experience across multiple disciplines, Grandmaster Sterling founded Martial Arts Academy in 1999 with a vision to create a world-class training facility accessible to all.

Holding black belts in Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu (5th Degree), Karate (7th Dan), and Judo (4th Dan), he has trained under legendary masters in Brazil, Japan, and Thailand. His competitive career includes numerous national and international championships.

Under his leadership, the academy has produced champions in multiple disciplines while maintaining a welcoming environment for beginners. His philosophy emphasizes that martial arts is a lifelong journey of self-improvement, not just physical combat.
